#  Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis | SQL + Python + Power BI

 Project Overview

Customer behavior analysis plays a crucial role in helping businesses understand purchasing patterns, customer preferences, and sales trends. This project analyzes a retail customer shopping dataset using **SQL, Python, and Power BI** to generate actionable business insights that can support marketing strategies, inventory planning, and customer engagement.

The project follows a complete data analytics workflow including data cleaning, SQL analysis, exploratory data analysis (EDA), dashboard creation, and business insight generation.

---
 Business Problem

Retail businesses collect large amounts of customer transaction data, but extracting meaningful insights from this data can be challenging.

The objective of this project is to answer key business questions such as:

* Which product categories generate the highest sales?
* How do customer demographics influence purchasing behavior?
* Which payment methods are most preferred?
* How do discounts and promotional offers impact purchases?
* What purchasing patterns can help improve business decisions?

#  Key Business Insights

The analysis revealed several important business insights:

* Clothing and Accessories are among the most frequently purchased product categories.
* Adult customers contribute the largest share of purchases.
* Customers using subscriptions tend to purchase more frequently.
* Discounts and promotional campaigns positively influence customer purchasing behavior.
* Digital payment methods are widely preferred over traditional payment methods.
* Customer ratings remain consistently positive, indicating overall customer satisfaction.
* Seasonal variations significantly influence purchasing patterns, suggesting opportunities for seasonal marketing campaigns.
